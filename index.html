About ICONIC STRUCTURE & DESIGN LTD.

ICONIC STRUCTURE & DESIGN LTD. (ISDL) was founded with a simple yet genuine pledge — to deliver quality homes for the residents of Dhaka city.

Our management recognized the immense potential of Dhaka and dedicated every effort to meeting its rapidly growing housing needs. With a skilled team of engineers, architects, and professionals, we focus on innovation, quality, and trust.
